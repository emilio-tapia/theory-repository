Recent studies show that voice assistants have become increasingly popular with 27% of the global online population using them on a monthly basis, and this number is expected to grow rapidly in the coming years.

Design Reference

However, the accuracy and naturalness of speech recognition and voice synthesis systems remain a significant challenge for widespread adoption. According to a report by the Stanford Open Voice Network, 44% of users experience issues with speech recognition accuracy, and 48% of users feel that voice assistants sound too robotic.

Voice Reference

Improving the accuracy and naturalness of these systems would significantly improve user experience and facilitate the widespread adoption of voice technology. In addition, voice technology has shown great potential for improving accessibility in various domains, such as healthcare and education, where hands-free interaction is crucial. By exploring approaches to enhance the capabilities of voice technology through machine learning, I hope to contribute to the advancement of this field and create more inclusive and accessible solutions for users.
